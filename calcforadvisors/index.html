Depending on the plan, you may be able to point a subdomain of your own domain (e.g. tools.yourfirm.com) to your white-label experience. Details can be provided when you get in touch.

Is client data stored?

Calculators can be used without clients creating accounts. If save/export features are enabled, data is handled according to our privacy and security practices—we don’t sell client data.
